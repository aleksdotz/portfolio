Project case

Momentum

Fitness & nutrition app concept with workout, nutrition, dashboard and onboarding flows.

Project context

Momentum is a mobile-first fitness and nutrition app concept. The app is designed to help users track their workouts and nutrition, and to provide them with a dashboard and onboarding flow to help them get started.

Design Process

I followed an iterative design process starting with user research to understand beginner needs and pain points, translating these insights into low- and high-fidelity designs, and continuously validating decisions through usability testing and feedback to refine the app’s simplicity, clarity, and effectiveness.

Onboarding context

The onboarding flow translates user data directly into a personalized plan. By building the process into clear steps, the input remains easy and the start of the app feels immediately relevant.

Design Process

The onboarding process is designed to quickly personalize the experience by guiding users through a few simple steps where they define their goals, preferences, and training habits. The inputs in this flow are directly based on key pain points and insights from user research, such as lack of structure, uncertainty about where to start, and difficulty staying consistent. By addressing these issues upfront, the app is able to generate a tailored plan that lowers the barrier to entry and provides immediate clarity and direction.
